ZENTORY Labs is a multi-asset quant research protocol. We operate:

## Why This Partnership Benefits Hyperliquid

### 1. Deeper HYPE Utility

Currently HYPE is a governance token with fee buybacks. Through ZENTORY:

- HYPE holders can stake HYPE in the ZENT Alpha Vault and earn systematic yield
- This creates a real yield use case for HYPE beyond governance and fee buybacks
- Every HYPE staked in the vault represents new TVL in the Hyperliquid ecosystem

### 2. Trader Onboarding

ZENT's Research Network attracts:

- Professional quant traders
- Algo trading firms
- Signal providers

These traders naturally use perpetuals exchanges. ZENTORY can serve as a funnel for Hyperliquid's perpetual trading business — quants who earn ZENT rewards need a venue to execute, and Hyperliquid is the natural destination.

### 3. Multi-Asset Vault

ZENT's vault is multi-asset: BTC, ETH, SOL, XRP, HYPE. The vault trades across crypto spot/premium, forex, and potentially equities long-term. This makes ZENT a unique differentiator for Hyperliquid vs. other DEXs — ZENT is one of the only protocols bringing multi-asset L1 staking + systematic algo trading to HyperEVM.

### 4. On-Chain Transparency

ZENTORY's vault strategy is fully verifiable on-chain. This brings:

- Regulatory credibility and institutional appeal
- New user segments (systematic investors, quant funds) to Hyperliquid
- ZENT's compliance posture strengthens Hyperliquid's narrative as institutional-grade DeFi infrastructure
