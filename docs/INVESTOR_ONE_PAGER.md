## The Solution

ZENTORY is a two-layer quant research and vault ecosystem on HyperEVM:

**Layer 1 — Alpha Vaults**
Deposit BTC, ETH, SOL, XRP into non-custodial ERC-4626 vaults. Earn yield generated by the ZENTORY trading system. No private keys surrendered. No counterparty risk.

**Layer 2 — The Signal Arena**
A social exchange for quant research. Quants stake ZENT on their own signals (Conviction Score). Followers auto-copy top quants. The Ghost Portfolio shows — on-chain, in real time — what following signals actually returned vs. holding BTC.

**The viral loop:** A shareable ZENTORY Score card for every wallet. Connect wallet → see your score → share it. This is the ORBT Protocol mechanic applied to actual financial products instead of just DeFi score theater.

## The Products

### Alpha Vaults
Non-custodial, ERC-4626 vaults (zBTC, zETH, zSOL, zXRP) on HyperEVM. Depositors earn yield from the trading system's execution. **Revenue:** 15% performance fee on vault yield.

### Ghost Portfolio *(unique to ZENTORY)*
The only on-chain verifiable proof of what following signals would have returned. Three lines: HOLD the asset, FOLLOW quant signals (ghost), ACTUAL vault performance. This is the product demo that drives organic shares.

### Conviction Scores *(unique to ZENTORY)*
Every signal carries a Conviction Score: how much ZENT the quant staked on it. 10,000 ZENT staked = signal weighted 10,000x. This reveals who actually believes in their research vs. who is spam-signaling. **No other platform has this.**

### ZENTORY Score Cards *(viral loop)*
Shareable reputation cards for every wallet. Proven viral mechanic (ORBT generated 70K shares). Drive organic growth at near-zero cost.

### Auto-Follow *(HyperEVM copy trading)*
Non-custodial copy trading. Follow a quant → your vault balance mirrors their signals. Stop anytime. No counterparty risk. **No one has done this on HyperEVM.**

### Volatility Brackets *(engagement engine)*
48-hour competitive tournaments when BTC moves >3% in 1 hour. Top 8 quants compete. Followers watch live. Winners get ZENT from the protocol treasury. Creates the appointment-TV moment that makes users come back every day.
