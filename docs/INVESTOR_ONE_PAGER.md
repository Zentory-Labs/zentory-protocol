# ZENTORY Labs — Investor One-Pager

## The Problem

Retail investors lose billions annually to information asymmetry — quant funds control sophisticated signals and execution that retail can never access. Meanwhile, on-chain yield is fragmented across dozens of protocols with no credible way to verify performance track records. The best quant talent is locked in proprietary firms; there's no public, permissionless way to reward open research.

## The Solution

ZENTORY Labs is a multi-asset quant research protocol on HyperEVM where users stake BTC, SOL, ETH, XRP, and HYPE into a non-custodial Alpha Vault that trades asset premium/discount dynamics, and quant contributors earn ZENT tokens for producing verifiable, on-chain track records of research accuracy.

## The Product

### Alpha Vault *(Revenue: 15% performance fee)*

Users deposit BTC, SOL, ETH, XRP, or HYPE and receive yield generated by the ZENT premium/discount trading algorithm. The algo exploits cyclical mispricing between asset pairs on HyperEVM DEXes. **Key differentiator:** non-custodial — users never surrender private keys. Staked assets are locked in user-controlled wallets; the algo executes via smart contract mandates only. Compare to Yearn (active since 2020, $250M+ TVL) or GMX (glitchy, centralized keeper risk) — ZENT targets a different niche: multi-asset DeFi-native quant yield with full transparency.

### Research Network *(Revenue: 10 / 25 / 50 / 100 ZENT/month subscription tiers)*

Quant contributors submit signals and research outputs on-chain. Accurate predictions earn ZENT rewards and a public, immutable track record. The best contributors feed into the **Zent Fund** — a curated quant strategy powered by top-ranked research. This creates a flywheel: better research → better fund performance → more ZENT demand → more quants join. Research is public and verifiable, unlike traditional quant shops where track records are self-reported.

### ZENT Token

| Property | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Utility** | Governance voting + premium research access + subscription fee payment |
| **Supply** | Fixed total supply; deflationary via buyback & burn |
| **Staking rewards framing** | Governance participation, not profit distribution |
| **Deflation** | 50% of protocol revenue (vault fees + subscriptions) used for ZENT buyback & burn |
| **Legal** | Utility token; geo-blocking implemented for US and other restricted jurisdictions |
